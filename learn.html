LEARN AT HOME

This page is full of information and knowledge that you can teach to yourself!

Social Media

These video tutorials will give you some basic understanding of the Social Media services Instagram, Twitter, Facebook, and Pinterest.

Instagram Guide for Beginners

Instagram is a photo and video-sharing social networking service owned by Facebook, Inc. This video will teach you how to setup and use Instagram.

Twiter Guide for Beginners

Twitter is a microblogging and social networking service on which users post and interact with messages known as "tweets". This video will teach you how to setup and use Twitter.

Facebook Guide for Beginners

Facebook is a social networking site that makes it easy for you to connect and share with family and friends online. This video will teach you how to setup and use Facebook.

Pinterest Guide for Beginners

Pinterest is an image sharing and social media service designed to enable saving and discovery of information using images and, on a smaller scale, GIFs and videos, in the form of "pinboards". This video will teach you how to setup and use Pinterest.

Google Products

These video tutorials will give you some basic understanding of the a few of the Google services. You will learn how to setup a Google Account, how to start with Gmail, Google Slides, Google Sheets, and Google Calendar.

Introduction to Google and Gmail

Gmail is a free email service developed by Google. This multi-part tutorial will give you all the information that you need to create a Google account and setup Gmail.

Introduction to Google Slides

Google Slides is a presentation program included as part of a free, web-based software office suite offered by Google within its Google Drive service. This video will teach you the basics of Google Slides.

Introduction to Google Sheets

Google Sheets is a spreadsheet program included as part of a free, web-based software office suite offered by Google within its Google Drive service. This video will teach you the basics of Google Sheets.

Google Calendar for Beginners

Google Calendar is a time-management and scheduling calendar service developed by Google. This video will teach you how to use Google Calendar.
