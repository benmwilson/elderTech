Frequently Asked Questions

What is elderTech?

ElderTech is a free service provided for senior citizens or the eldery to learn more about technology. We offer 'Technology Training' services for free for those that need them most. We also have a wide variety of technology related information that you can teach yourself at home!

 How can you help me?

Here at elderTech, we strive to teach tech. If you have been wanting to get your hands on technology but do not understand how to work it or how to use it, we can help! With our wide range of 'Learn at Home' information as well as our online 'Technology Training', we can help you learn! 

What does it cost?

The services provided by elderTech are free. All services are carried out by volunteers.
