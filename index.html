Frequently Asked Questions

What is elderTech?

ElderTech is a free service provided for senior citizens or the eldery to learn more about technology. We offer Technical Support and Training services for free for those that need them most. We also have a wide variety of technology related information that you can teach yourself at home!

 How can you help me?

Here at elderTech, we strive to solve any technology related issue you throw at us. We will do everything in our power to resolve the issue, and maybe teach you something along the way as well.

What does it cost?

The services provided by elderTech are free. All services are carried out by volunteers.
